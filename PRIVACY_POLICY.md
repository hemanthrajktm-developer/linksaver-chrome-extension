# Privacy Policy for LinkSaver Chrome Extension

**Effective Date:** December 26, 2024  
**Developer:** Hemanth Kumar M

## Data Collection and Usage

LinkSaver is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here's what you need to know:

### What Data We Collect
- **Saved Links**: URLs, titles, and notes you choose to save
- **Organization Data**: Folders, tags, and categories you create
- **Usage Preferences**: Your settings and interface preferences

### What Data We DON'T Collect
- We do NOT collect any personal information
- We do NOT track your browsing history
- We do NOT send data to external servers
- We do NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- We do NOT share data with third parties

### How Your Data is Stored
- All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API
- Data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it
- No cloud storage or external databases are used
- You have complete control over your data

### Data Access and Control
- **View**: Access all your data through the extension dashboard
- **Export**: Download your complete data as a JSON file
- **Delete**: Remove individual links or clear all data
- **Import**: Restore data from previously exported files

### Permissions Used
- **Storage**: To save your links and preferences locally
- **Active Tab**: To read page information when you choose to save a link

### Data Security
- Data is stored using Chrome's secure storage system
- No network requests are made except for fetching website favicons
- All processing happens locally on your device

### Changes to Privacy Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the extension and on our distribution channels.

### Contact
For any privacy-related questions or concerns, please contact the developer.
